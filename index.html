Classes

Ballet Level 1 - teen & adult

  • Time: Mondays, 6 – 7pm, June 6 – 27 (4 classes)
  • Location: All Saints by-the-Sea lower hall (110 Park Drive)
  • Cost: $40 for 4 classes (or $10 for a single class). This is a 4-class series, but you can sign up for individual classes if you can’t attend all 4.
  • Instructor: Kathryn Luttin
  • Class description: This class is for absolute beginners through to those with a couple years of ballet training. We’ll practice the basic movement patterns upon which ballet is built, and learn some simple combinations to get you moving. This class is intended to be inclusive of a diverse range of abilities and ages. You’ll be encouraged to do what works for your body at this time. Wear comfortable clothes that don’t restrict your movement. Ballet slippers are best, but socks work. The floor is not sprung, so we won’t be jumping. But we will have fun!

How to Register

Register by emailing cedarpointdance(at)gmail.com and sending an e-transfer to the same email. Registration is not complete until payment is received. You must register at least 2 days prior to each class. Class size is limited to 8. If the class is full, we’ll let you know, and your e-transfer payment will not be accepted.

Cancellations: Full refund if you need to cancel due to illness, otherwise, cancellation requires 3 days notice for a refund.

About Kathryn Luttin

Kathryn Luttin is a professionally-trained ballet dancer. She danced with the National Ballet of Canada as an apprentice, but left professional dance at the age of 20 due to injuries. She later gained a Master’s degree in Physical Therapy. After years away from dance, she has recently ‘returned to the studio’, to both train and teach. She currently teaches after-school ballet for the GISPA dance program at the Gulf Islands Secondary School.
