Step, Hop & Shimmy! A celebratory dance show, bringing together dancers from Salt Spring and beyond

  • June 3, 2023, 6:30 pm
  • Venue: All Saints by-the-Sea church sanctuary (110 Park Drive, Salt Spring Island)
  • Show length: approximately 1.5 hours, with one intermission
  • Tickets: $10. Any ticket revenue beyond the show expenses will be donated to a charity.

Hosted by Cedarpoint Dance, and organized by Kathryn Luttin and Sarah Allen, this celebratory dance show features dancers from several communities and a range of dance styles, from ballet to belly dance and more. Our common thread is a love of movement and music.

How to buy tickets:

  1. Email your full name and the number of tickets requested to cedarpointdance(at)gmail.com. We will reply to confirm if tickets are available.
  2. Pay by e-transfer to cedarpointdance(at)gmail.com (no security question required). If you are unable to do e-transfers, please email us and we can make other arrangements.
  3. Tickets will be held for you at the door. Please arrive at 6:15 pm on show night to pick up your tickets.

Tickets are very limited. Tickets will be available at the door only if available – we highly recommend purchasing tickets in advance.

About Kathryn Luttin

Kathryn Luttin is a professionally-trained ballet dancer. She danced with the National Ballet of Canada as an apprentice, but left professional dance at the age of 20 due to injuries. She later gained a Master’s degree in Physical Therapy. She has taught ballet at the GISPA dance program at the Gulf Islands Secondary School and at the Rainwood Dance Studio on Salt Spring Island.
