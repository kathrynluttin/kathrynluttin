Ballet Level 1 - teen & adult
Time: Mondays, 6 – 7pm, June 6 – 27 (4 classes)
Location: All Saints by-the-Sea lower hall (110 Park Drive)
Cost: $40 for 4 classes (or $10 for a single class). This is a 4-class series, but you can sign up for individual classes if you can’t attend all 4.
Instructor: Kathryn Luttin
Class description: This class is for absolute beginners through to those with a couple years of ballet training. We’ll practice the basic movement patterns upon which ballet is built, and learn some simple combinations to get you moving. This class is intended to be inclusive of a diverse range of abilities and ages. You’ll be encouraged to do what works for your body at this time. Wear comfortable clothes that don’t restrict your movement. Ballet slippers are best, but socks work. The floor is not sprung, so we won’t be jumping. But we will have fun!
Register by emailing cedarpointdance@gmail.com and sending an e-transfer to the same email. Registration is not complete until payment is received. You must register at least 2 days prior to each class. Class size is limited to 7. If the class is full, we’ll let you know, and your e-transfer payment will not be accepted.
