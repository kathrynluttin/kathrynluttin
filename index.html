Cedarpoint Dance

cedarpointdance(at)gmail.com

Our purpose is to provide dance classes on Salt Spring Island for a variety of ages and levels. We are working on getting a dedicated studio space at the new CRD-run SIMS building starting in Summer 2022. The goal is to provide a fully-equiped dance studio that is available for rent to local dance teachers to hold their classes. Stay tuned as this unfolds!

We are using the 'Royal We' here... Cedarpoint Dance is run by just me - Kathryn Luttin!

Get our updates

Sign up for our email list to get notifications of new classes. Email cedarpointdance(at)gmail.com and say "add me to your email list".

How to Register

Register by emailing cedarpointdance(at)gmail.com and sending an e-transfer to the same email. Registration is not complete until payment is received. If possible, please register at least 2 days prior to each class. Class size is limited to 8. If the class is full, we’ll let you know, and your e-transfer payment will not be accepted.

Cancellations: Full refund if you need to cancel due to illness, otherwise, cancellation requires 3 days notice for a refund.

About Kathryn Luttin

Kathryn Luttin is a professionally-trained ballet dancer. She danced with the National Ballet of Canada as an apprentice, but left professional dance at the age of 20 due to injuries. She later gained a Master’s degree in Physical Therapy. For the past 2 years, she has taught after-school ballet for the GISPA dance program at the Gulf Islands Secondary School. She also taught ballet at the Rainwood Dance Studio from 2018 - 2021.
